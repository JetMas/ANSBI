Health Services - Emergency Medical Services (EMS)

An emergency is a medical condition for which immediate attention is necessary to prevent death or serious impairment of the health of an individual. Severe chest pain, a broken leg, or fainting are just some examples of emergencies.


For all emergencies, call (575) 854-2525 or Socorro General Hospital at (575) 835-8370. If EMS is on a run and staff does not answer the phone, hang up and call Socorro General Hospital. Should you wish to leave a message with the EMS staff a recording is available in which the staff can return calls.


Hours for EMS are 24/7 all 365 days.
