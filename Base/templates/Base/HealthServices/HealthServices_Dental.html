Health Services Dental

The Alamo Health Center provides a variety of Dental services. Alamo children are screened for cavities and the need for dental sealant. Several Dental Health promotion/disease prevention activities are carried out throughout the year. There are annual diabetic dental exams that are completed as an important part of the continuum of care.


Besides the filling of teeth for cavities, the staff can remove teeth as needed and if additional services are necessary, a referral may be done.
