Scholarships Available for Alamo Community who graduated from Alamo Community School

JAMES A APACHITO MEMORIAL SCHOLARSHIP

James A. Apachito believed in higher education for the Alamo Navajo Community. He believed that each and everyone of the youth who graduates from the Alamo Navajo School has the potential for higher education and to be successful in their journey to becoming self-sufficient. It is this strong belief that led to the creation of this memorial scholarship to assist Alamo Navajo College students with the financial means to successfully complete a four-year degree and to become successful in the their career endeavors. It is the hope that they will return to Alamo to apply their knowledge and wisdom to benefit the continuing development of the Alamo Community.

Award Eligibility

  • Alamo Navajo Community School Graduate
  • Certificate of Indian Blood (CIB)
  • Scholarship Application
  • Classified as a junior in an accredited post secondary institution
  • GPA of 2.75 or greater
  • Two letters of recommendation from an advisor, counselor and/or faculty member
  • 500 word essay

Scholarship Information

The James A. Apachito Memorial Scholarship is awarded to college students classified as a college junior or above and registered in an approved accredited post-secondary institution.

Application deadline will be announced so applicants will have ample time to complete the application process.
